Rampart Ridge's Trail Directions

Trailhead directions From Elbe at the junction of SR 7 and SR 706, head east on SR 706 passing through Ashford and reaching Mount Rainier National Park at 14 miles. Continue west on park road for 6.5 miles to Longmire and trailhead parking near the Wilderness Information Center.

Trailhead facilities: privy, water, visitor center, park concession
